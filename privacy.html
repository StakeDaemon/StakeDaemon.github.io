Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect minimal user information necessary to provide staking services: wallet addresses, staking activity, and transaction details. We never request unnecessary personal details such as names, phone numbers, or passwords.

2. How We Use Data

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating staking rewards.
  • Ensuring smooth withdrawals and deposits.
  • Maintaining platform security and preventing fraud.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data. Limited data may be shared only if legally required by regulatory authorities.

4. Security

All sensitive interactions (deposits, withdrawals, rewards) are protected with strong encryption and blockchain security standards. Users are advised to keep their private keys safe, as StakeDaemon never stores them.

5. Cookies

Our platform may use cookies to improve user experience, but we do not track or profile users for advertising.

6. User Rights

Users can request removal of their staking records from our database (except mandatory blockchain records). Contact our support team for privacy-related requests.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
